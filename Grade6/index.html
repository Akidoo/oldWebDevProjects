There are various positive impacts being kind has on society. Below, there are some images of people being kind to one another, or slogans that support kindness.




In this image, you will observe an elderly lady giving something to a homeless man.


Being kind is important for various reasons. It helps build good relationships
with others, people will like you more. Aiden Spencer states that “Finding out
what kindness is first is a good start, if people are kind to one another, there
is no reason to be unkind to one another.” Recent scientific evidence also suggests
that being kind positively affects the happiness and mental health of a person.
Spencer also claims that being kind is very similar to an act of selfishness, and
it is good to be selfish to a certain extent. Furthermore, Spencer says that the
world has made a lot of progress with being kind since World War 2, but there
is still plenty of room for improvement.


The peace symbol can also serve as a symbol of kindness.



Being kind isn't only limited to being kind to each other. There are plenty of ways on which you
can be kind to the environment, such as participating in a beach cleanup, Recycling items, and
binning litter. It is important to be kind to and care for our environment because all creatures
will die if we don't. Even if nobody is hosting a beach cleanup near you, it is very easy to do one on
your own. Just grab a few garbage bags and a pair of tongs and set off to a nearby beach!










































































What you can do

There are many things you can do. You can set little objectives
for yourself, like going a week without using plastic products. You
could also go for a scavenger hunt with your friends, but the objective
is to recycle as many objects as possible. It's even better that way,
because you don't need to set up the items, because they are garbage.
As for the No plastic for a week goal, you could use, for example, glass
and wood products, however, refrain from using too many wood products
as more trees will die, and that isn't being kind to nature. You could also
set a challenge for yourself where you have to participate in a
certain amount of cleanups of beaches, streets, etc.
